Appointment support

Reliable company for appointments and outings

Badwi Care & Companionship can accompany people to appointments so the journey and waiting time feel less stressful.

Support before, during and after

Appointment support can include reminders, getting ready to leave, travelling together, waiting with the person and returning home safely after the appointment.

  • Company travelling to appointments
  • Support with confidence and reassurance
  • Waiting together before or after appointments
  • Helping the day feel more organised
  • Family updates where agreed
