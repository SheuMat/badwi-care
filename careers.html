Careers

Become a companionship and support worker

Badwi Care and Companionship Service is interested in people who are kind, reliable, respectful and good at helping others feel comfortable.

A role built around friendly support

Support workers help with companionship, cleaning, meals, shopping, appointments and community support. The role is about communication, patience and practical help.

You should enjoy spending time with people, listening well and helping someone feel more independent and connected.

What we look for

Personal qualities matter

Friendly attitude

A warm manner that helps people feel relaxed and respected.

Reliability

Turning up when agreed and communicating clearly with clients and families.

Good communication

Listening, explaining clearly and sharing helpful updates when needed.

Compassion

Understanding that small acts of kindness can make a big difference.

Respect

Supporting people in a dignified way that protects choice and independence.

Practical help

Confidence helping with companionship, cleaning, meals, shopping, appointments and community support.
