Companionship at home

Choosing to have a caring companion support you at home is an important decision, and we're here to make that choice easier. At Badwi Care and Companionship Service, our dedicated care assistants provide compassionate, reliable support to help you maintain your independence, dignity, and quality of life.

We offer flexible care arrangements, competitive pricing, and personalised support tailored to your individual needs and preferences. Whether you require occasional companionship or more regular assistance, our team is committed to helping you live comfortably and confidently in your own home.

  • Conversation and emotional wellbeing support
  • Playing games, cards or doing hobbies
  • Company during quiet parts of the day
  • Encouragement to keep routines going
  • Reassurance for family members

Our Caregivers

All our staff are fully qualified, experienced, and committed to delivering person-centred care with professionalism and kindness. They are able to assist with companionship, social engagement, daily living support, and general wellbeing needs.

For your peace of mind, all staff have:

  • Valid right-to-work documentation
  • Enhanced DBS checks
  • Professional references and reference numbers available
  • Relevant care qualifications and training
  • Up-to-date mandatory training compliance
