Shopping support

Help with shopping and everyday essentials

Badwi Care and Companionship Service can support people with shopping lists, local shops, groceries and friendly company while out in the community.

Practical shopping help

Shopping support can help someone stay independent while making the task feel less tiring or stressful. It can also reassure families that essential items are being managed.

  • Preparing shopping lists
  • Accompanying clients to local shops
  • Help choosing groceries and essentials
  • Support carrying light bags
  • Keeping family members updated where helpful
