Sitting service

Reliable company while family carers take time out

Badwi Care & Companionship can spend time with someone at home so family carers can rest, work, attend appointments or handle other responsibilities.

Calm, friendly reassurance

The sitting service is designed to feel simple and reassuring. A support worker can provide company, conversation, activities and general wellbeing reassurance during the visit.

  • Friendly company at home
  • Conversation, games or quiet presence
  • Support with light routines during the visit
  • Reassurance for family carers
  • Flexible visits arranged around family needs
