Wellbeing checks

Friendly check-ins that reassure families

Badwi Care and Companionship Service can visit to check someone is comfortable, connected and coping with everyday routines.

Simple reassurance

Wellbeing checks are useful when family members cannot always be nearby. Visits can include conversation, checking general comfort, preparing a drink or light snack, and sharing agreed updates.

  • Friendly check-ins at home
  • Conversation and reassurance
  • Support with simple daily routines
  • Preparing a drink or light snack
  • Updates for family members where agreed
